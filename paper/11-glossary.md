# Glossary

- **MCP**: Model Context Protocol
- **AI Hub**: TODO: define the intended Government / Local Authority service pattern.
- **Analysis**: The authors' interpretation of facts and reported opinions. Must be labelled and should not introduce unsupported evidence.
- **Fact**: A claim directly supported by a cited source.
- **Reported opinion**: A claim about what a person, organisation, community, or vendor says or believes.
- **Residual risk**: Risk that remains after stated mitigations.
- **Source register**: A structured source note in `sources/` or `wiki/sources/` used before drafting prose.
