# Authoring Contract

## Required Labels

Every substantive section must separate:

- **Facts**: source-backed claims.
- **Reported opinions**: attributed claims about what people or organisations say.
- **Analysis**: the paper's reasoning from facts and reported opinions.

## Drafting Rules

- Do not write the full report until the relevant source notes are accepted.
- Do not introduce first-use evidence in the executive summary.
- Do not cite the imported Deep Research report directly in final prose.
- Convert imported-report claims into source-note entries before using them.
- Use direct quotations sparingly and keep them under 25 words.

## Context Rules

- Start from the section's `Source Boundary`.
- Load only the relevant source-note file(s).
- Use the wiki note that matches the task: citations, source discipline, or acceptance criteria.
- Update `wiki/progress/phase-log.md` when a phase materially changes.
