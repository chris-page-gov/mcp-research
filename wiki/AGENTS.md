# MCP Research Wiki Operating Rules

This folder is a guidance wiki for the MCP research paper. It is not a second paper draft and should not contain unsupported claims.

## Purpose

- Keep context small and task-relevant for future agents.
- Preserve source, citation, and acceptance rules outside the paper prose.
- Track progress and quality gates without bloating `README.md` or section files.

## Boundaries

- Do not draft the full report inside `wiki/`.
- Do not add uncited facts to guidance pages.
- Do not use `TODO-...` citation keys as evidence; they mark blockers.
- Keep pages short enough to load selectively.

## Navigation Contract

- `index.md` is the wiki entry point.
- `guidance/` contains durable authoring rules.
- `progress/` contains current plan and phase history.
- `templates/` contains reusable skeletons for sources and sections.
- `evals/` contains ideas and future scripts for measuring wiki effectiveness.
- `data/` contains generated or machine-readable state.

## Current Bias
